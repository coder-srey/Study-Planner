Study Planner

The NITC Study Planner is an efficient WebApp for students to plan their daily academic activities effectively.With a lot of busy schedules, students can prioritize their scheduled activities with help of this system easily. The problem of forgetting personal daily goals,due dates or submission deadlines, test timings etc can be managed effectively by this system by generating notifications and setting reminders to the students. This system also helps students to remain productive throughout the entire day.
